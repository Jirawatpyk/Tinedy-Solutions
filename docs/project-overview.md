# Tinedy CRM - Project Overview

> Generated: 2026-01-22 | Version: 0.0.0

## What is Tinedy CRM?

Tinedy CRM is a **booking management system** designed for service businesses in Thailand. It enables businesses to manage customer bookings, staff assignments, and payments through a modern web interface.

## Key Features

### Booking Management
- Create, edit, and track bookings
- Assign bookings to individual staff or teams
- Status flow: pending → confirmed → in_progress → completed/cancelled
- Calendar and weekly schedule views
- Smart booking with conflict detection

### Customer Management
- Customer profiles with contact information
- Booking history and analytics
- Tag-based organization
- Customer avatars

### Staff Management
- Staff profiles with auto-generated staff numbers
- Skills tracking
- Team organization with team leads
- Staff availability management
- Performance tracking

### Role-Based Access Control
| Role | Access Level |
|------|-------------|
| **Admin (Super Admin)** | Full system access |
| **Manager (Admin)** | Operations without hard delete or settings |
| **Staff** | Personal work only |

### Communication
- Internal chat system with file attachments
- In-app notifications with realtime updates
- Email notifications (booking confirmations, reminders)

### Reporting
- Revenue analytics
- Booking statistics
- Staff performance metrics
- Export to CSV/PDF

### Payment
- Payment slip upload
- PromptPay QR code generation
- Payment status tracking
